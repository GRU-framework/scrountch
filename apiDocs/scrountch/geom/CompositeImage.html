Package: scrountch.geom

[Groovy] Class CompositeImage

        • scrountch.geom.CompositeImage

        • instances of this class are mainly supposed to hide other graphic Objects. they are displayed in front of them and transparent "holes" let see underlying objects. They can be used also to create compound images built from different images on top of each other.
